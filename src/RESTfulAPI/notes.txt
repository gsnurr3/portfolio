EFC Scaffolding (from src/RESTfulAPI):

    AppDbContext:
    dotnet ef dbcontext scaffold "Server=localhost;Database=PortfolioApp_Dev;Trusted_Connection=True;TrustServerCertificate=True;" Microsoft.EntityFrameworkCore.SqlServer --context AppDbContext --context-dir Persistence --output-dir Domain/Entities --no-onconfiguring --use-database-names --data-annotations --schema dbo --table Departments --table Patients --table Payments --table InsuranceProviders --table PaymentMethods --force

    LogDbContext:
    dotnet ef dbcontext scaffold "Server=localhost;Database=PortfolioApp_Dev;Trusted_Connection=True;TrustServerCertificate=True;" Microsoft.EntityFrameworkCore.SqlServer --context LogDbContext --context-dir Persistence --output-dir Domain/Entities --no-onconfiguring --use-database-names --data-annotations --schema dbo --table RequestLogs --force

Initial EF Core Migration (from portfolio):

    AppDbContext:
    dotnet ef migrations add Baseline_App --context AppDbContext --output-dir Persistence/Migrations/App -s src/RESTfulAPI -p src/RESTfulAPI

    LogDbContext:
    dotnet ef migrations add Baseline_Log --context LogDbContext --output-dir Persistence/Migrations/Log -s src/RESTfulAPI -p src/RESTfulAPI
